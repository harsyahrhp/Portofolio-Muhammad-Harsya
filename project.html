Project Title

Medstore App

Year Accomplished

2022

Project Description

Medstore™ is a drugstore, and like any drugstore, it's a store where you can buy medicines, make-up, and other things like toiletries and face masks. Attracts thousands of locals since it was founded. Due to the pandemic situation, even though more people are in need of medicine, it is difficult to come and it is also a high risk situation for customers to browse in-store and for Medstore employees to meet people, so Medstore™ is really taking a loss. There is a solution to overcome this problem, namely by creating an order kiosk display, where the display system is expected to help customers to be able to place orders without having to enter Medstore™ and make it easier for Medstore™ to inform about promos that may be present at certain times.
